Admission Procedure

Step-by-step guidance to support your application to YJIS.

In this section

    1. Inquiry: Submit the online inquiry form and attend an information session.
    2. Application: Upload school reports, recommendation letters, and the student profile.
    3. Assessment: Students complete an age-appropriate academic assessment and interview.
    4. Decision: Admissions decisions are shared via email within two weeks.

    Our team is available to assist with translations, documentation, and special considerations. Please reach out if your family requires additional support.
