    The Journey to Youngji International School: Where Your Story Begins The Youngji International School Admissions Team welcomes you and stands ready to accompany your family throughout this new journey, from initial inquiry to final enrollment.

    Our process is fundamentally transparent and student-centered, designed to look beyond scores to truly understand each applicant's unique story and potential. First, follow the links provided to explore essential details: find information on scholarships, review the specific requirements for entry, and note the crucial key dates.

    Next, we invite you to experience our community firsthand. Schedule a campus visit or arrange a virtual conversation to envision life here and connect personally with our team. We are here to guide you every step of the way as your child begins their chapter with us.
