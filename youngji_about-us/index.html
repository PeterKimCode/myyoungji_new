About Youngji International School

We cultivate compassionate leaders who think globally, act responsibly, and champion lifelong learning across cultures.

In this section

    Youngji International School was founded on the belief that every student deserves an inspiring environment where curiosity is celebrated. Our community welcomes families from diverse backgrounds and cultivates meaningful connections between students, educators, and the world.

    Through rigorous academics, service learning, and arts integration, our learners discover their strengths and develop the confidence to pursue their aspirations.

    Why families choose YJIS

    • Authentic international curriculum tailored to the needs of modern learners.
    • Holistic support services encompassing counseling, health, and wellbeing.
    • Rich co-curricular programs including robotics, athletics, and performing arts.
