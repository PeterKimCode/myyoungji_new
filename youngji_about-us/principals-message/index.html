    Dear families and friends,

    Youngji International School began as a dream to create a joyful space where learners feel seen, heard, and empowered. Each classroom is a testament to the dedication of our teachers and the curiosity of our students.

    As you walk our halls, you will find voices of collaboration, empathy, and innovation. We invite you to partner with us as we nurture scholars who are courageous, compassionate, and committed to positive change.

    Warmly,
    Dr. NAG SHIN KIM, Founder
